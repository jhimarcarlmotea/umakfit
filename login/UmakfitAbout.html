Our Mission

Shaping well-rounded athletes and educators

To attain its vision, the CHK will provide a comprehensive and cross-disciplinary curriculum that is responsive to the needs of the students and other stakeholders. It shall promote excellence in teaching and management of sports and wellness education.

Our Vision

Leading fitness assessment across Philippine universities

CHK envisions to be recognized as one of the exemplary leading educational institutions in the wellness, sports, and recreation industry. It commits to produce physically educated, health and wellness advocates and service-oriented practitioners who will deliver the competencies of teaching and the demands of the corporate industry.

Our Story

How UMakFIT came to life

"

We built UMakFIT because fitness data should empower students — not collect dust in a spreadsheet.

— UMakFIT Development Team
U MakFIT was born from a simple observation: fitness assessment in universities was often manual, time-consuming, and provided limited insights into student health trends. Faculty members spent countless hours recording data, calculating scores, and generating reports, while students rarely had access to their historical fitness data or personalized improvement recommendations.
Recognizing this gap, a team of passionate developers, health professionals, and educators from the University of Makati came together with a shared vision: to create a comprehensive digital platform that would transform how fitness assessment is conducted in academic settings.
Today, UMakFIT stands as a testament to innovation in health technology, serving students across programs and providing valuable research data that contributes to the understanding of youth fitness in the Philippines. We continue to evolve, incorporating feedback from our community and staying at the forefront of fitness assessment technology.
