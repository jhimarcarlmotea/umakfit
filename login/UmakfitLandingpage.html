Platform Features

Everything you need to excel in fitness

Comprehensive tools built for UMak students and faculty to assess, track, and understand physical fitness with scientific precision.

01
📋
Standardized Fitness Tests

Scientifically-backed assessments including cardiovascular endurance, strength, flexibility, and body composition with built-in timers and calculators.

02
📈
Real-Time Progress Tracking

Detailed analytics, progress charts, and personalized insights that help you understand your improvement over time with beautiful visualizations.

03
🎯
Personalized Results & Feedback

Instant feedback with age and gender-specific norms, percentile rankings, and actionable recommendations tailored to your profile.

04
💻
Intuitive Interface

Step-by-step instructions, clear visual guides, and streamlined tools that make fitness testing accessible and simple for everyone.

05
📊
Advanced Analytics

Comprehensive reporting for educators and researchers with group comparisons, statistical analysis, and exportable data for academic use.

06
🔒
Secure & Private

Enterprise-grade security protecting your fitness data. Integrated with UMak credentials for seamless, trusted access to your profile.
