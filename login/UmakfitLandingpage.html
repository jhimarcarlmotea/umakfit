Track Your Fitness Journey with UMakFit

The comprehensive fitness assessment platform designed for University of Makati students and faculty. Monitor your health, complete standardized fitness tests, and track your progress over time.

Comprehensive Fitness Assessment

Everything you need to complete, track, and analyze your fitness journey in one powerful platform

Standardized Fitness Tests

Complete scientifically-backed fitness assessments including cardiovascular endurance, strength, flexibility, and body composition tests with built-in timers and calculators.

Real-Time Progress Tracking

Monitor your fitness journey with detailed analytics, progress charts, and personalized insights that help you understand your improvement over time.

Personalized Results & Feedback

Get instant feedback on your performance with age and gender-specific norms, percentile rankings, and actionable recommendations for improvement.

User-Friendly Interface

Intuitive design with step-by-step instructions, video guides, and easy-to-use tools that make fitness testing accessible for everyone.

Advanced Analytics

Comprehensive reporting system for educators and researchers with group comparisons, statistical analysis, and exportable data for academic purposes.

Secure & Private

Your fitness data is protected with enterprise-grade security. Integrated with UMak credentials for seamless and secure access to your fitness profile.
